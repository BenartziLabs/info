Benartzi Labs

Benartzi Labs is a behavioral science and applied AI research firm focused on improving decision-making, product design, and customer experiences. Our work combines insights from behavioral economics with advanced machine learning to help organizations rapidly test, iterate, and improve ideas before deploying them in the real world.

Our core platform, (GenP), enables faster and more rigorous research by simulating how representative populations (digital twins) are likely to respond to products, messages, and experiences.

Our Services and Products

Generative Populations (GenP)

GenP is Benartzi Labs proprietary platform for simulating high-fidelity population responses. It integrates behavioral science, statistical modeling, and modern AI techniques to generate realistic, population-level insights without relying on traditional survey fieldwork.

GenP is commonly used for:

Market research and message testing
Product and feature evaluation
User experience and journey optimization
Sales and go-to-market experimentation

By combining behavioral theory with rapid iteration, GenP allows organizations to test hypotheses, compare alternatives, and refine strategies in days rather than months.

How It Works

GenP constructs simulated populations that reflect the characteristics, constraints, and incentives of real-world groups (for example, consumers, professionals, or specific demographic segments).

These populations are calibrated using:

Behavioral science research
Statistical validation techniques
Publicly available and non-personal data sources
Client-provided materials such as marketing personas

Once calibrated, GenP can be used to run simulated surveys, A/B tests, and scenario analyses to inform decisions and guide improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you use personal data or PII?

No. Benartzi Labs does not use personally identifiable information (PII) or personal data about real individuals in its generative population work.

What data sources do you rely on?

GenP typically relies on a combination of:

Publicly available data (such as census and aggregate demographic data)
Academic and behavioral science research
Client-provided materials (e.g., marketing copy, product descriptions, or messaging)

We do not ingest or process customer databases, user-level data, or sensitive personal information.

Is GenP intended to replace real users or customers?

No. GenP is designed to support early-stage testing, hypothesis generation, and rapid iteration. It complements rather than replaces real-world testing by helping teams focus resources on the most promising options.
